ABOUT ME

As a data analyst, I specialize in transforming raw data into actionable insights. My expertise includes data cleaning, statistical analysis, and data visualization using tools such as MS Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. I have a track record of using data analytics to drive decision-making and improve operational efficiency in my previous roles. I am available for short-term projects as well as part-time, longer-term engagements. If you need a dedicated professional to help with your data analysis or web development needs, feel free to reach out!
